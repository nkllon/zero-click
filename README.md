# zero-click

Zero-click search, traffic, and discovery impact — data and references.

Searches that resolve without a click (e.g. answers in SERP, AI overviews) reduce traffic to the open web and shift value from the click layer to answer/agent layer. This repo collects references, metrics, and notes on zero-click share, publisher impact, and downstream effects.

## Topics

- **Zero-click share**: % of queries that end without a click to external sites (e.g. SparkToro, SEO studies).
- **Publisher traffic**: Clicks per 1k searches to the open web; trends by region and vertical.
- **AI overviews**: Rollout and zero-click correlation (e.g. Google AI Overviews, other answer-in-SERP).
- **Corpus link**: Tied to the [internet-biz corpus](https://github.com/nkllon) narrative on click-for-dollars vs walled gardens and Gen AI erosion of the discovery layer.
